About Kyoo
Queueing management solutions

Waiting and falling in line has always been a dilemma. With Kyoo, we are offering you a business solution to integrate in your daily operations and to improve customer satisfaction.

Premium Features

We will provide you with a device that does everything for you.

Walk-In & Online Queueing

Reducing the need for a physical line while enriching the customer wait-time experience.

Custom QR Slip

A cost effective way to connect mobile users to business' website, ads and social media platforms.

SMS Notifications

Instant updates that keep customers informed and be engaged with the queueing process.

Manage Reservations

Hassle-free process to optimize customer service and maximize potential sales 24/7.

With Kyoo, it allows businesses to smartly manage long lines by letting customers to queue virtually via remote queueing or with the aid of a handheld device. Customers may get real-time queue status by scanning the QR codes from generated queueing slips and SMS notifications.
